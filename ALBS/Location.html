Getting to the Airport:

There are a multitude of ways to get to the Airport, You can reserve parking through NCP, book a taxi from a number of taxi companies in the local area or use First Buses 77 Service from Glasgow, Buchanan

Getting to the Lounge

After Security, Make your way through duty free and past boots. To the left of the currency exchange head up the ramp and through the doors to our luxury lounge.
