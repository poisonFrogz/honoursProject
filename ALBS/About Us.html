The Lounge:

Located upstairs from the main terminal, The Flying Scotsman Lounge is the perfect place to relax while you wait for your flight. The lounge bar offers a wide range of wines and beers along with non-alcoholic options. While our buffet offers everything from bar snacks to hot meals. For the red eye travellers the coffee machines are free to use as much as you would like.

Terms and Conditions:

Alcohol is limited to 4 drinks per person with the opportunity to buy more.

Football Strips are prohibited.

Guests are allotted 3 Hours from the time of booking.

Children and Bags must not be left unattended.

Lounge Staff reserve the right to refuse entry based on these terms.
